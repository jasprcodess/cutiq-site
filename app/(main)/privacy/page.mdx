## Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 21, 2026

CutIQ ("CutIQ," "we," "our," or "us") provides AI-powered hair analysis, hairstyle previews, routines, and chat-based hair guidance on iPhone. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app handles, how it is used, and what choices you have.

## Overview

CutIQ is designed to help you analyze your hair, preview hairstyles, and track progress over time. The app uses a combination of on-device storage, Apple iCloud, and our backend infrastructure. We do not sell your personal information and we do not use third-party advertising trackers.

## Data We Collect and How It Is Handled

### Photos and image content

- **Source photos** (selfies or photos you select for style generation): These are held in device memory only for the duration of the request. They are converted to a compressed format, sent to our backend for AI processing, and released from memory afterward. Source photos are never written to disk by the app and never stored on our servers.
- **Front analysis photo**: When you complete a hair analysis, your front-facing photo is saved locally on your device and synced to your private iCloud (CloudKit) account so your analysis results can display it across sessions and devices.
- **Other analysis photos** (top, side, hairline): These are combined into a single composite image, sent to our backend for analysis, and then released from memory. They are not stored on your device or in iCloud.
- **Generated hairstyle images**: AI-generated results are saved locally on your device and synced to your private iCloud (CloudKit) account. If the auto-save setting is enabled (on by default), generated images are also saved to your device photo library with a CutIQ watermark.

### Hair analysis and routine data

The app stores your analysis results (score, hair type, texture, face shape, routine steps, weekly schedule), check-in history, routine preferences, streaks, and settings. This data is stored locally on your device and synced to your private iCloud (CloudKit) account.

### Profile and onboarding data

During onboarding, the app collects your selected face shape, age range, gender, and other preferences. This is stored locally and synced to your private iCloud (CloudKit) account.

### Identifiers

CutIQ uses an iCloud / CloudKit-based user identity. No email address, phone number, or password is collected for app use. App-specific identifiers are used for syncing data and verifying secure requests.

### Purchase and subscription status

If you subscribe, the app verifies your entitlement through Apple in-app purchases. Transaction metadata (such as a signed transaction token) is sent to our backend for server-side subscription verification. We do not receive your payment card information.

### Chat messages

When you use the AI chat feature, your messages and conversation history (up to the last 10 turns) are sent to our backend for processing. Chat messages are not stored on our servers or in iCloud. They exist only in device memory for the duration of your chat session.

### Usage-limiting and security metadata

Our backend processes limited request metadata for App Attest verification, subscription validation, rate limiting, and abuse prevention. Rate-limit counters are held in server memory only and are not persisted.

### Support communications

If you email us, we receive the information you include in that message.

## How We Use Data

We use data to:

- provide hairstyle generation, hair analysis, and chat features;
- save your app state and progress across sessions;
- sync your data through your private iCloud / CloudKit storage;
- verify subscriptions and secure app requests;
- enforce usage limits and prevent abuse;
- respond to support requests.

## Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only as needed to operate the app, including with the service providers listed below, or when required by law.

## Third-Party Services

1. **Apple CloudKit / iCloud** by Apple — used for syncing app data including your profile, analysis results, front analysis photo, and generated images. All CloudKit data is stored in your private database, accessible only to your iCloud account. [Apple privacy policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/)

2. **Apple In-App Purchase / App Store** by Apple — used to process subscriptions and verify purchase status. We do not receive your payment card information. [Apple privacy policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/)

3. **Supabase** by Supabase, Inc. — used for backend edge functions that handle App Attest verification, hair analysis requests, hairstyle generation requests, and chat requests. Image data you submit for analysis or generation passes through Supabase to reach the AI model provider. Our backend does not persist image data or chat messages. [Supabase privacy policy](https://supabase.com/privacy)

4. **Superwall** by Superwall — used for paywall presentation and entitlement handling. Receives app-specific user identifiers and subscription state. [Superwall privacy policy](https://superwall.com/privacy)

5. **AI model providers accessed through our backend** — used to produce hair analysis results, hairstyle generation images, and chat responses. Image and text content you submit for these features is forwarded through our backend to the model provider. These requests are stateless on our end.

## Data Retention

- Data in your iCloud / CloudKit account remains until you delete it through the app, delete app data from your device, or manage it through your Apple account settings.
- Locally stored data (images, analysis results) remains on your device until you delete it through the app or uninstall the app.
- Our backend does not persist user data beyond the duration of individual requests. Rate-limit counters reset automatically.
- Support emails are retained as long as reasonably necessary to respond and maintain support history.

## Security

We use reasonable safeguards including encrypted network transport (HTTPS/TLS), Apple App Attest verification, server-side subscription verification, rate limiting, and input validation. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## Your Choices

You can:

- stop granting camera or photo-library access through your iPhone settings;
- turn off auto-save to photo library in the app settings;
- delete individual generated images or all data through the app;
- disable notifications through iPhone settings or within the app;
- manage or cancel subscriptions through Apple;
- contact us at [support@cutiq.app](mailto:support@cutiq.app) to request help related to your data.

## Children's Privacy

CutIQ is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## International Transfers

## App Tracking Transparency

CutIQ does not track you across apps and websites owned by other companies for advertising purposes as defined by Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework.

## Notifications

If you enable reminders, CutIQ schedules local notifications on your device for daily hair tips, routine reminders, and weekly analysis check-ins. These are managed through Apple's notification system and your device settings.
